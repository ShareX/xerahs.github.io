Object-Based Editing

Unlike traditional paint tools that bake changes into the image, XerahS Editor treats every annotation as a separate object on its own layer.

Powerful Flexibility

This non-destructive approach gives you complete control over your screenshots:

Movable Objects

Every arrow, text box, or shape is an independent object. You can select, move, resize, or delete them at any time even after you've drawn other things on top.

Non-Destructive

Because the original image remains untouched underneath, you can accidentally cover important details with an annotation and simply move it later to reveal them again.

Z-Ordering

Bring important annotations to the front or send background elements to the back. Layer management ensures your visual hierarchy is exactly how you want it.

Re-Edit Capability

Did you make a typo in a text box? Need to change the color of an arrow? With multi-layer support, you just click the object and change its properties. No need to undo and redraw.

Project Saving: You can even save your work as a project file to come back and edit the text or layers days later.
