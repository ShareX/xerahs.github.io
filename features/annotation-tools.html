Annotating Screenshots

XerahS includes a rich set of annotation tools to help you express your ideas clearly and concisely.

Drawing Tools

Basic Shapes

  • Rectangle / Ellipse: Draw precise shapes with customizable borders and fill colors.
  • Line / Arrow: Add straight lines or directional arrows.
  • Freehand: Sketch freely with varying brush sizes.
  • Standard: Add standard annotations like steps (1, 2, 3...) or stickers.

Text & Callouts

  • Text: Add text labels with full font customization.
  • Speech Balloon: Create comic-style speech bubbles.
  • Step: Numbered steps for creating tutorials or guides.

Utility Tools

Privacy & Highlight

  • Blur: Sensitively blur out personal information.
  • Pixelate: Apply a mosaic effect to hide details.
  • Highlight: Draw attention to specific areas with a translucent overlay.
  • Smart Eraser: Intelligent deletion tool.

Modification

  • Crop: Trim the canvas to a specific selection.
  • Cut Out: Remove a section of the image completely.
  • Magnify: Create a zoomed-in loupe effect over a detail.
  • Spotlight: Darken the surrounding area to spotlight a specific region.
Tip: All tools support undo/redo and layer management, allowing you to edit annotations even after they are placed.
